Overview

The Napoli INdAM Trimester 2026 focuses on models and methods of applied mathematics in multiscale and multiphysics continuum mechanics.

The programme includes three thematic summer schools, a general workshop on instabilities, mini-courses, and outreach activities in Naples.

The Trimester runs from 1 May to 31 July 2026.
