Weekly Seminars & Activities

Week 19–23 May

Miniworkshop: Recent developments in the model theory of fields

Wed–Fri
Monte Sant'Angelo campus
View event
Week 26–30 May

A "gros" topos for algebraic geometry of exponential rings

Mon 15:30
Matías Menni
Aula F

We will introduce the main properties of the "big" étale topos, and then adapt the construction to the case of exponential rings. (Work in progress.)

Week 26–30 May

Modular Zilber–Pink for generic varieties

Wed 11:00
Sebastian Eterović
Sala Riunioni Level 2

We will present a variant of the Zilber–Pink conjecture in the modular setting, focusing on unlikely intersections with a generic special subvariety.

Week 26–30 May

Hensel minimality and Tate uniformization

Fri 11:00
Sebastian Eterović
Sala Riunioni Level 1

We will discuss a Tate uniformization theorem for Henselian valued fields.

Week 26–30 May

Fields with commuting and iterative operators

Fri 12:30
Omar León Sánchez
Sala Riunioni Level 1

We will discuss the model theory of fields equipped with commuting automorphisms and derivations, including iterative Hasse–Schmidt derivations.

Week 2–6 June

Generic differential automorphisms in positive characteristic

Wed 12:00
Omar León Sánchez
Sala Riunioni Level 2

We will discuss generic differential automorphisms of fields of positive characteristic and connections with difference-differential fields.

Week 2–6 June

Valued difference fields and existentially closed models

(seminar time TBA)
Sala Riunioni Level 2

Valued difference fields are fields equipped with a valuation and a compatible automorphism. I will talk about joint work with Jan Dobrowolski and Francesco Gallinaro on existentially closed valued difference fields.

We prove that they admit an equivariant section of the valuation, characterise amalgamation bases for valued fields of residue characteristic , and deduce that they are in the sense of positive logic.

Week 9–13 June

On elimination of imaginaries in ultraproducts of p-adics

Tue 11:00
Pietro Freni
Sala Riunioni Level 2

I will discuss elimination of imaginaries for ultraproducts of p-adics, focusing on recent results in the direction of definable sets and applications.

Week 30 June – 4 July

Transseries for dummies

Tue 12:00
Vincenzo Mantova
Sala Riunioni Level 2

I will introduce transseries by explaining their basic construction and some of their model-theoretic features.

Week 30 June – 4 July

Local stability in NIP theories

(seminar time TBA)

Local stability has been used to solve counting problems in combinatorics and o-minimality. We will discuss a general framework for local stability in NIP theories and present some recent results. (Joint work with L. Krupiński and A. Milovich.)

Week 7–11 July

On the open core of the p-adics

Thu 12:00
Anna Dmitrieva
Sala Riunioni Level 2

A one-dimensional definable subset of (Q_p, +, ×) is a finite union of points and open balls.

Week 14–18 July

Definable topologies in tame expansions of fields

Tue 11:00
Pablo Cubides Kovacsics
Sala Riunioni Level 2

We will discuss definable topologies in tame expansions of fields, focusing on definable completeness and separation properties.

Week 14–18 July

Tame expansions of real closed fields

Fri 11:00
Elisa Giordano
Sala Riunioni Level 2

We will discuss the model theory of tame expansions of real closed fields and applications to definable groups.
